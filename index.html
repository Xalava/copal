International Cooperation Plateform




WELCOME TO COPAL, A COLLABORATIVE PLATFORM CONNECTING UNIVERSITIES

DESCRIPTION



Copal is a website allowing Professors to create an international teamwork experience for their students.

The website focuses on helping Professors to find partners and to set up the collaboration. Students’ work is eased by the integration of collaborative tools and stimulated by potential outcomes

Resulting portfolios, connections and feedbacks are designed to facilitate exchange programs



DATA




8,302 French Students in the US / 17,210 US Students in France



9% of US students study abroad during their Bachelor



60% of exchanges are short-term (eight weeks or less)


KEY FEATURES

For Professors

  • Find partners
  • Set up working languages and deadlines
  • Overview students with a dashboard
  • Retrieve and grade final projects

For Students

  • Find teammates matching their interests and skills
  • Integration of collaborative tools
  • Contact Universities and Alumni for potential exchanges
